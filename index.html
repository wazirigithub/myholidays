Top 3 Places to Visit In Tanzania

Tanzania is blessed with so many stunning cities which makes it a great place to wander around. Blessed with exotic landscapes, the country has amazing topography to admire at. Check out the best cities and their must-visit attractions below.

Kikuletwa Hot Springs

Best Time to Visit: November to March

The hidden gem along Arusha and Moshi is a beautiful oasis surrounded by little jungles waiting for you to enjoy swimming before or after climbing Mount and Safari. While recovering from multi-day tours and hiking trips, a relaxing day tour in Tanzania is one of the best things to do. So enjoy the journey to the Chemka Hot Springs, also known as the Kikuletwa Hot Springs. Just 1 hour from Arusha and 30 mins from Moshi Town, this is one of the hidden gems of Tanzania that you need to visit. The crystal clear water in this jungle oasis where you can relax all day and enjoy rope swings, tire tubing, and some high jumps out of the trees.

Ngorongoro Crater

This captivating journey takes you to the Ngorongoro Conservation Area, where you can witness the beauty of nature and wildlife. This day tour is the perfect choice for a condensed African safari experience. The Ngorongoro Conservation Area is a testament to nature’s magnificence. It’s not just a wildlife sanctuary; it’s a place where nature and wildlife coexist harmoniously. The area’s diverse ecosystems are a vital part of its charm. While wildlife often takes centre stage, Ngorongoro’s flora is equally captivating. Explore the unique plants and trees that thrive in this environment.

Zanzibar Beach

Whether for a honeymoon, anniversary or a simple holiday for two, this classic beach holiday has been created for couples looking to celebrate. Featuring superb accommodation, you have nearly a week to explore the Island of Tanzania’s most celebrated and sumptuous tropical island retreat.
